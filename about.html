About Me

I always had a strong interest towards drawing and computers. After middle school I studied graphic design. Upon finishing designer school I went to University Of Szeged to study computer engineering.

Currently I am a freelance web developer / illustrator / graphic designer.
In my spare time I enjoy gaming, cartoons and skateboarding. I am also interested in character design and motion design.
My favorite is vector design but I like experimenting with other things too. I really love to learn new things and I'm a quite open minded and creative person. I like challenges and I aspire to be a better every day. I really like working in a team but I can also solve problems on my own.
