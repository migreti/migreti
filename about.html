About Me

I always had a strong interest towards drawing and computers. After middle school I studied graphic design. Upon finishing designer school I went to University Of Szeged to study computer engineering. Currently I am a freelance web developer / illustrator / graphic designer.

In my spare time I enjoy gaming, cartoons and skateboarding. I am also interested in character design and motion design.
My favorite is vector design but I like experimenting with other techniques too. I constantly try to keep pushing my boundaries and broaden my horizon. I love to learn new things and I'm a quite open minded and creative person.
I consider myself a great teamworker but I'm also able to solve problems independently.
