WHO AM I?

Hi! My name is Greta and I'm an illustrator and designer. I always had a strong interest towards drawing and computers. I also study computer engineering and I'm a freelance frontend developer.

In my spare time I enjoy music, games, cartoons, skateboarding and photography. I'm interested in character design and motion design too.
I really like experimenting with different illustration styles and I constantly try to keep pushing my boundaries and broaden my horizon. I love to learn new things and I'm a very open minded and creative person.
