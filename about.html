Who is Greta?

Hi! My name is Greta and I'm a full-time UI designer and a freelance illustrator. Sometimes I also do frontend development.

Since I was little I had a strong interest towards drawing and computers. In my spare time I enjoy taking care of my plants, listening to music, playing games, watching cartoons and photography. I really enjoy coming up with new ideas and I like to experiment in all creative fields but I'm mainly into visual arts.
At day I enjoy creating cool user interfaces. At night I enjoy doing illustrations. I constantly try to keep pushing my boundaries and broaden my horizon. I love to learn new things and I'm a very open minded and creative person.
